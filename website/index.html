Process

How the bot works

Every signal goes through a strict multi-stage pipeline before reaching your Telegram.

01
Market Scan
Scheduler fires every N minutes. Fetches OHLCV candles for all watched symbols from Binance (spot + futures). Anti-lookahead: all calculations use closed_candles[:-1]; current price from the live candle for display only.
02
Indicator Calculation
RSI, MACD, EMA stack, Bollinger Bands, ATR, volume ratio, Open Interest delta, funding rate, Coinbase premium, liquidation levels — all computed in parallel across 15M, 1H, 4H, 1D timeframes.
03
Signal Fusion & Scoring
Each detector votes (bullish / bearish / neutral). Votes are weighted by confidence and combined into a master score. Ecosystem detector checks correlation with BTC/ETH. Market regime filter applies regime-specific thresholds.
04
Gate Checks
News gate, macro gate, sentiment gate, whale gate, and symbol blacklist are checked. Signal is dropped silently if any gate is closed. Only high-conviction, environmentally-validated signals pass through.
05
Trade Plan & Delivery
Entry mode (MOMENTUM_NOW / RETEST_WAIT), TP1/TP2/TP3 targets, stop-loss, and risk block are computed. Signal is dispatched to Telegram with optional chart image. Pending signal recorded for outcome tracking.
06
Outcome Tracking & Learning
Next scan checks if pending signal hit TP or SL. SL hit → auto mini-backtest → lesson derived → learning engine updated. Symbol memory updated. Telegram notification sent if signal was wrong.
